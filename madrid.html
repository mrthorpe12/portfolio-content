Madrid

From Barcelona, we traveled via train to Madrid, the capital of Spain. Home to over 3 million residents, Madrid's stately avenues, grand parks, and world-renowned museums give it a cosmopolitan feel.

While in Madrid, one must-see destination is the Plaza Mayor. Built in the late 16th century during the reign of Philip III, the Plaza Mayor is a vibrant hub within the old city center, home to upscale restaurants and shops.
